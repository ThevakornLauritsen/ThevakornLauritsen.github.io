Summary

Current side project which is developing into a potential start-up idea.
Three of my friends and I started brainstorming this game back in Dec, 2022, and began pre-production in Jan, 2023
From there on it started escalating into production planning, estimated costs, and how many people and years we would need to finish this game and start the company.
This portfolio will go through my thought processes from start to finish, how far we have come and the current lessons from this project.

Process

It first started out us laying out an pre-production planning. This is where we want to evaluate what kind of game and genre we want to do.
This was long process, as we had to consider the several stages for production from demo, to recruitment, funding, and creating the company.

Lead Level Designer

Making greybox designs for the levels. Coordinate with artists and game designer/director on what to iterate.

Lead Narrative Design

Primarily working on the story and how to translate it to various components of the game. This can be setting, music, location and storyline.

Game Producer

Administration work such as production planning, deadlines, pipeline refinement, contracts and research for funding and investments.
